# Feature: Restaurant Operations Panel

## What
Restaurant staff can access a management panel to monitor operations and manage resources such as orders, tables, and related operational views.

## Why
Restaurants need a centralized workspace to keep service activity organized and to manage day-to-day operations without leaving the platform.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Staff can sign in to the restaurant panel
- [ ] Operational views are available for order and table workflows
- [ ] Staff actions are limited according to their access level
