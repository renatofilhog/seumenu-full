# Feature: Digital Menu Browsing

## What
Customers can open a restaurant menu, browse highlighted and categorized items, and inspect product details before deciding what to order.

## Why
This gives restaurants a digital storefront that is easier to access, easier to update, and better suited to self-service ordering.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Customers can view active menu items for a restaurant
- [ ] Customers can browse highlighted items and categories
- [ ] Customers can open product details before adding items to an order
